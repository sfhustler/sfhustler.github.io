How It Works

    • Our expert crew will install our custom cameras at appropriate locations in your lot.

    • Our proprietary AI technology processes the images in real time to detect parked cars, read the license plates and check for any violations.

    • When violations are found, a picture along with appropriate info is produced for a citation to be issued and mailed.

  • Vehicle parked in the wrong location.
